How it works

Three simple steps to reunite students with their belongings

1
Someone finds an item
A student or staff member finds a lost item on campus and submits it here with a photo and location.
2
It gets listed instantly
The item appears on our platform in real time, visible to all UJ students searching for their lost belongings.
3
Owner finds & collects
The owner searches, finds their item, notes the location, and goes to collect it from safekeeping.
