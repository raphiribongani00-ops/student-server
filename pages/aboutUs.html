Our Story

Why Find My Stuff Campus was built

🎓 Built for Students

A problem every student knows too well

Find My Stuff Campus is a platform that helps students find their belongings that they lost at campus. The developer lost a bank card and only realised after 2 weeks that he had lost it. He didn't know where to start looking.

Whilst on a quest to find his bank card, he saw many bank cards, ID cards, and driver's licences at safekeeping that people had lost and never came to collect. Maybe they didn't even know that what they had lost had been found.

💡 He saw a great need to solve the problem of lost and found items using a real-time digital platform — and so Find My Stuff Campus was born.
